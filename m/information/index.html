About the Ministry

The Ministry of Information & Broadcasting serves as the official communication channel of Cupx Nation. We ensure citizens stay informed about government decisions, new services, policy changes, and important notices across all subdomains.

This ministry powers the citizen dashboard, official notices, and future features like electionx, keeping transparency at the heart of the digital nation.

Core Responsibilities

  • → Citizen dashboard and personalized updates
  • → Digital broadcasting and information dissemination
  • → Management of electionx and public polls
  • → Transparency and right to information
