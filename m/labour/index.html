About the Ministry

The Ministry of Labour & Employment manages employment opportunities, issues experience certificates, and ensures worker welfare across Cupx Nation. All job postings in your ecosystem (amazx, ridex, railway, airline, etc.) are regulated and verified through this ministry.

Every salary, benefit, and employment record is linked to your citizens table and paid through your Cupx bank.

Core Responsibilities

  • → Jobsx – Official Employment Portal
  • → Issuance of employment & experience certificates
  • → Worker welfare and benefits schemes

  • → Labour law compliance for all businesses
