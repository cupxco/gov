About the Ministry

The Ministry of Home Affairs is one of the most important pillars of Cupx Nation. It oversees citizenship matters, maintains public order in the digital space, handles emergency responses, and coordinates with other ministries for national security.

This ministry works closely with the Ministry of Identity & Citizenship to ensure every citizen's record in the `citizens` table is accurate and secure.
